Customer Disputes Choropleth Map

This interactive visualization is a choropleth map of the United States that displays the distribution of customer disputes across different states for a selected company. The map is designed to help businesses identify geographic hotspots for customer disputes, allowing them to tailor their customer service strategies and resource allocation accordingly. The visualization is built using the Dash framework in Python, which enables the creation of interactive and responsive web applications. It leverages the powerful data visualization capabilities of the Plotly library to generate the choropleth map.
